About Me

Hi there! I'm Shreyal Sharma, a Computer Science major at NJIT with a passion for AI learning and innovation.

When I'm not coding, you'll find me immersed in the captivating world of novels or participating in hackathons with my fellow Women in Computing club members.

As the Business Manager at Vector, I blend my love for writing with my passion for technology through the Vector newspaper.

My commitment to giving back to the community led me to serve as an e-board member of Kids Who Code, empowering the next generation with essential digital skills.

In addition to my academic pursuits, I'm also a math tutor, helping students excel in their learning journey.

Join me as I continue to make a positive impact, one line of code at a time!
